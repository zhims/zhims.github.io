Yao Zhang - Quantum


Quantum science explores the fundamental nature of matter, energy, and information at the smallest scales. Quantum mechanics describes the behavior of particles like electrons and photons, introducing principles such as superposition, entanglement, and uncertainty. Quantum field theory (QFT) extends these ideas, combining quantum mechanics with special relativity to model particles as excitations of underlying fields, forming the foundation of the Standard Model. Quantum computing applies quantum principles to process information, utilizing qubits to perform complex calculations exponentially faster than classical computers in certain tasks. These fields together shape cutting-edge research in physics, technology, and computation, driving innovation across disciplines.
