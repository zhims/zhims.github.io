Yao Zhang - Magnetohydrodynamics, MHD


Magnetohydrodynamics (MHD) is the study of the behavior of electrically conducting fluids¡ªsuch as plasmas, liquid metals, or saltwater¡ªin the presence of magnetic fields. MHD combines principles from both fluid dynamics and electromagnetism, exploring how these two aspects interact. It is an important field in astrophysics, engineering, and fusion research.
