Biography

  • Yao was born and raised in a rural village near Weishan Lake in northwestern Jiangsu, China. He spent his entire childhood there and completed his early education within the local school system.

  • After earning his B.S. and M.S. degrees in Mathematics from Shenzhen University, Yao worked at a semiconductor company near Lihu Lake in Wuxi and ran a small street stall. In the fall of 2022, he married a doctor, marking a significant milestone in his personal life.

  • Yao served as a research assistant at the Chinese Academy of Sciences before earning his Ph.D. in Astronomy in September 2024. He is currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at Ningbo University, focusing on artificial intelligence and its applications in science and engineering.
